Made it possible with tech- Online Classes

Author - Riya

So, lets talk about the most trending topic of lockdown, which has probably changed the scenario for many students as well as teachers drastically- none other than online classes. This brings us to the analysis of its pros and cons. Well, in such a crucial time, online teaching was a necessity, but I don’t believe it can replace the old school teaching ways.
It is indeed very clear that, students are probably going to end up having big specs resting over their noses after this lockdown and teachers are too having a hard time adapting this new concept of teaching. But these internet handles have proven beneficial for the students (I know most of you will agree) and also for other citizens.
They no longer have to wait for vacations, I guess and it’s a lot easier to maintain records of what’s being taught in the classes, as internet is smart enough to pamper you. But, let me make this clear, this is not going to last forever and eventually you are going to end up carrying those bulky bags on your shoulders, reminiscing those old lockdown days. So, better be ready.
This lockdown has eventually taught us the importance and range of how technology is embodied in nearly every aspect of our lives. The field of education has also expanded its reach to international level. Today’s network is very efficiently accommodating millions of students and teachers on the same platform. This is a just a minute show of what power internet has in today’s world.
I know old ways can’t be replaced, but in the nearby future, technology can definitely result as an asset in the times of urgencies. The present scenario sets a very clear example of this proposition.
