AI is the future or destroyer of technology?


Author - Tushar Verma

We all have seen Hollywood films like The Terminator 2 ,mad Max, Robocop et cetera . while seeing these films on the big screen fill us with awe and intrigue that can't be defined because it showcases the strength and wonder that artificial intelligence is capable of achieving. And it's not very far away into the future when we might see Technologies that are fully controlled by AI. We are already living in an age where our smartphones provide suggestions and make choices for us. we get suggestions on Netflix based upon our viewing pattern. this ability of the software or machine in general to learn, adapt and improvise is what we can call artificial intelligence.



We are already living in a world Where a artificially intelligent humanoid named Sofia is living amongst us, being a celebrity in her own right. now this begs the question whether artificial intelligence in the long run will be a boon or a curse to the human race. Some of the world's biggest tech tycoons and some of the world's smartest brains have already assessed the dangers that artificial intelligence can pose to the human race.





They have readily agreed upon not to weaponize the world with artificial intelligence because they are very well aware that if artificial intelligence becomes sentient then there's nothing that humans can do to prevent Armageddon. it might sound a bit far fetched in the present world scenario but indeed it is a possibility of the future. Imagine a scenario not very far away from the present time where an AI controlled Nuclear missile thinks of another country as an active threat to its existence. this might trigger a nuclear launch by the artificial intelligence and one can only imagine the magnitude of destruction and the laws of life it will create. humans just to show that they are the smartest beings on Earth would have created something that will take them 50 years back in the past. there can be a literal machine versus humans war in the future if humans continue to pat themselves on their back for every step they take to make AI even more smarter and inch a bit towards making it even more sentient. humans have and can continue to live grow and develop without the help of artificial intelligence because there is nothing smarter or more creative than a human mind.



To sum it up I would say that artificial intelligence should not become an essence of our daily life and in no way should it be made smart enough to become sentient and take decision on its own because eventually the key of control should always remain in human's hand.
