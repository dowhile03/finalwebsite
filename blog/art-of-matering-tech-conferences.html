The Art of Mastering tech conferences

Author - Riya Shishodiya

Hola Tech Nerds! It seems, you have planned to showcase your skills in a big tech conference. But, will your planning make you the victor? We understand, most tech enthusiasts are kind of introverts, and confined in their own zone of laptops and other devices. But, sometimes they also have to come out of their comfort and presents their proposals in front of the like-minded people with sheer confidence and deep knowledge of their subject. Tech conferences can be national, or international, but regardless, they are a source of inspiration and a bucket full of new ideas for the beginners. One of the such big conferences is the WebSummit, which is a source of attraction for tech enthusiasts, as well as big companies like IBM, Microsoft, pinterest, which come to invest. But, don't worry we have got you covered.

Here are the top 5 skills you should master, to stand out in the most remarkable conferences.

  1. 1. Creation The purpose of attending the conference should be crystal clear. You should be well-prepared with the idea you want to present and have deep knowledge regarding your concept. People should be able to notice that you have done good research on your idea, and is a practical approach.


  2. 2. Sequence - When one is prepared with the concept, he/she should focus on the order in which they should arrange their keypoints. Hence, the goal of your presentation should be presented in a clear and effective way in the beginning, so the audience has something to hold on to. Secondly, you should focus on how the concept is practically applicable. The last part is very vital, as here you will try to persuade your audience and turn the tables in your favour.
  1. 3. Style/gestures- A presentation is not supposed to be longer, to be effective and persuasive. Try to minimize your speech, using phrases and focusing on the key-points of your idea. Its never harmful to stay within the deadline, instead, exceeding it tends the audience to lose interest. So, tick-tock, one eye always on the clock.


  2. 4. Time Management- Well, you are going to a tech conference, its obvious that you will meet a lot of new people, who are experienced and well-established in the industry. So, be all eyes and ears and jot down everything you need to remember! Also, for such opportunities, you should be all prepared with a short and highly persuasive monologue to pitch in.
