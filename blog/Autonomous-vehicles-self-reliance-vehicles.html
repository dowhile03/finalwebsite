Autonomous vehicles- self reliance vehicles

Author - Somya

It is like an imagination that we see vehicles moving on its own. But today, with the advancement of technology, we can really see cars moving themselves.
An autonomous vehicle, in the auto- pilot mode, can reach to the predetermined destination. It uses various in- vehicle technologies and sensors. Human control while driving is not required while driving and even human passenger is not required.

These vehicles rely on sensors, complex algorithms, machine learning and much more for maintaining speed, identifying surroundings, and reaching to the right place. There are 6 connectivity requirements which are needed to make a full self driven vehicle.
They are:

  1. Redundant and real time architecture
  2. Requirement of very high speed data connection
  3. Connectivity of vehicle with the external world
  4. We can not negotiate with the driving quality and reliability of the vehicle
  5. Approach towards safety is required
  6. Miniaturized structure is required for new generation design

By overcoming big stones of obstace like monitoring weather, checking traffic rules ,merger of artificial and emotional intelligence and right use of lidar and radar, it has proved to be biggest innovation ever
With help of sensors and radar autonous cars create a map and maintain it in relation to its surroundings and vehicles. It’s complex software controls the vehicle’s opeartions of brake, steering and accelaration and on the other side video cameras detect other vehicles, blockage and pedestrians on the road.
A complex algorithm machine is a boon to nature aswell because it reduces the carbon dioxide emmition by 80% all over the world and can also reduce the percentage of accidents taking place due to unconcious driving by people on road.

The top companys providing above mentioned technology are listed below:

  1. Waymo
  2. General Motor’s Cruise
  3. Ford motor company’s Agro AI
  4. Elon Musk’s Tesla
  5. China’s Baidu

The cars with such immense features are now coming to the maket at very high costs but there is not much time left that we will see such developments in an adequate budget. Critical thinking by upcoming generation and engineers is the need of hour today
