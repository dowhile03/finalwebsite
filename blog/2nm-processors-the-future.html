1.) How big an impact is due to a 2 nm processor?

The majority of the computer users are using Windows O.S which has the latest microprocessors ranging from (10 nm to 7 nm). These latest 2 nm processors are 45% faster than the 7 nm chips and they require 75 % less energy than the 7 nm processors. This breakthrough will help in accelerating advancement in areas such as 5g, 6g, autonomous vehicles and space exploration. IBM could put 50 billion transistors on a chip the size of a fingernail.





2.) Advantages of this new 2 nm processor

In these new 2 nm processors electrons have to travel less compared to previous generations microprocessors then the energy to operate the transferring of electrons will be less due to which less heat will be produced. Some major advantages are -
a.) This chip will quadruple the cell phone life.
b.) It will improve the self-driving car.
c.) It will drastically speed up your laptop performance.





3.) Quantum computing in future

IBM has already made a Quantum computer named Quantum Q. This new quantum computer is 158 million times more powerful than our latest supercomputers. It will take 10000 years for supercomputers to compute that much amount of data that Quantum computers can compute in one minute. Quantum computers could run 30 trillion of operations per seconds while the supercomputer can only run up to 200 quadrillion of operations per minute. These Quantum computers are in the initial stage but after 10 -15 years quantum computers can replace our supercomputers.
