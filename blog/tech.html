Technology - Shaping Our Future

By Riya Shishodia
If we go through the pages of our history and see how evolution has changed the purpose and mode of living, we will notice that technology has played a vital role and served us with many new innovations which made wonders possible. There were times when travelling from one place to another was a struggle. But with advancement, we invented wheels and then, vehicles, which made it an easy task. With technology, we are achieving goals which once were not possible. It has definitely served as an asset for everyone.. Moreover, technology has also contributed in provision of innovations, which have served as a cure for many problems. Technology is a vast source of knowledge and information. Still, there is so much to discover, that the lifestyle can drastically change within decades. In the present scenario, everything is being associated with artificial intelligence and wireless connectivity, which can in turn lead to the usage of automated devices. Work culture is transforming at a rapid rate which is beneficial for the fellows. Tech has made it all possible and will continue to empower the human race.
