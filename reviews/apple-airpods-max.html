Apple Airpods Max Review



    Detailed description :



    The Apple AirPods Max are the most hotly-anticipated headphones of 2020, having been the subject of rumor and speculation for two years. And now when they are finally out they have really created a hype in the market about themselves. So lets first take a look about the positive side of these headphones. The main highlight right from the moment you take it in your hands is its build quality, its made from stainless steel which means that it feels slightly heavy but it doesn’t feel so when you wear it, thanks to its super stable weight balance. They do look rather special compared to the competition, with a build quality that feels suitably premium for the price. Apple AirPods Max come with a headband made from a knitted mesh fabric that's designed to reduce on-head pressure. It comes with brighter color options, which include, silver, sky blue, green, and pink. Then comes one of the best thing this headphone has when compared to any other option available in the market i.e., its noise cancellation feature. The noise cancellation provided by the Apple AirPods Max is very strong, and we'd say it's on par with its competitors. Each ear cup features three outward-facing microphones to detect environmental noise, while one microphone inside the ear cup monitors the sound reaching your ear. Apple says the AirPods Max use computational audio to continuously adapt their noise cancellation performance based on "the headphone fit and movement in real time".

    Like the AirPods and AirPods Pro, the AirPods Max come with the Apple H1 chip in each earcup, which features no less than 10 audio cores to allow for Adaptive EQ, active noise cancellation, Transparency mode, and Spatial Audio. Released as part of iOS 14, the Spatial Audio feature first came to the AirPods Pro, and works for content in 5.1, 7.1 and Dolby Atmos, which positions sound all around you within a virtual sphere. If we talk about the sound quality of the headphones we can surely say that no easily out beat the competition. The audio quality offered by the Apple AirPods Max is simply outstanding. They come with a wide, well-balanced soundstage that leaves plenty of room for each instrument to really sing – pair that with cool extra features like automatically pausing your music when you remove the headphones and Spatial Audio, and you've got yourself a very special pair of cans. Apple says the AirPods Max provide up to 20 hours of high-fidelity audio, talk time, or movie playback, which ordinarily wouldn't seem a huge amount; however, given that this is with Active Noise Cancellation enabled, it's a pretty respectable figure.The best over-ear headphones will usually hit around 30 hours of battery life, although many very good models only make it to around 20 – either way it's enough for a full day or two's use, depending on how wedded they'll be to your ears. Connectivity comes courtesy of Bluetooth 5, and as such, pairing is fast and stable.

Somethings against:


• Very expensive (60k INR)
• No 3.5mm audio port
• Limited features for Android
• Ineffective carrying case
