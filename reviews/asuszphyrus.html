ASUS ZEPHYRUS G14

By Hrishabh Singhal

Specifications:
  1. Available from AMD Ryzen 5H to Ryzen 9HS 4000 series each
  2. Nvidia graphic cards from entry level 1650 to premeium RTX2060 Max Q
  3. Ram options of 8(upgradable to 24gb) and 16 gigs(upgradable) DDR4
  4. Available in 512 and 1TB super fast ssd storage
  5. Also gpu memory is GDDR6,fastest in the market for VRAM
  6. PORTS: two USB 3.0 ports and a USB type-C port, power connector and HDMI port
  7. Display : 1080p 120Hz and 1440p 60Hz with 323 nits of brightness level 14 inch pantone validated(perfect colors) display
  8. Fingerprint scanner available
  9. Anime matrix on the top of lid

OUR OPINION

This laptop is the is one of the best laptops for every kind of user. As the name suggests a gaming laptop this is far lighter and thinner compared to other laptops available in this category. To this benchmark of light and thin category the body is made of magnesium and aluminium alloy which certainly gives a premium look and is obviously strong. Coming to audio it is loud enough with speakers on both lower and upper part of the main laptop body. It also has drop hinge display which uplifts the back part of the keyboard slightly to bring it in a more comfortable position. Coming to heat management it performs significantly well even on heavy gaming when compared to other gaming laptops, keeps the body cool and also does sounds noticeably louder. It gives a battery life of around 10hrs on normal usage and 4-5hrs on gaming.
It’s the smallest gaming laptop available in the market and is surely considerable if you are looking for power with portability. display is rock solid and audio robust. Its an ultimate deal if you are planning to invest a good amount in laptop. Yes it is slightly over priced, but then every other laptop in india is because of government import duties.

THINGS TO HAVE A LOOK AT:
  1. No webcam because of very thin bezzels, so you have to buy separately
  2. Slight keyboard backlit issues in moonlight white model but the other is completely fine

PRICING:

Available at:
84-85k for base model with Ryzen5, 8gb ram, gtx1650 4gb Vram, 512gb ssd storage without anime matrix

To 160k for the top model Ryzen9HS with 16gb ram, rtx 2060 Max Q 6gb Vram, 1TB ssd storage and with anime matrix.
